Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to our website. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data at this moment. All data provided by you is meteoroid movement data.

2. Data Usage

The data you provide is used solely for generating graphs and new data on this website. Cached images of these graphs are stored temporarily and automatically deleted within 10 minutes after the last view.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Cached data is stored for 10 minutes. Links to generated graph images are publicly accessible to anyone who has the link. Please ensure you share links responsibly.

5. User Rights

As we do not collect personal data, there are no user rights related to data access, modification, or deletion.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on this website.

7. Policy Updates

We currently do not anticipate updates to this Privacy Policy. If updates are made, they will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
